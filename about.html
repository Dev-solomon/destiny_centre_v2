ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE IN CHRIST

We are a community of believers committed to worship, discipleship, and service. We exist to love God, love people, and share the Good News of Jesus Christ.

Hebrews 10: 24-25

OUR CORE BELIEFS

The foundation of our faith rests on the unchanging truth of God’s Word, shaping how we live, love, and serve.

1

Faith in God’s Word

We believe the Bible is the living Word of God, inspired and timeless, guiding every aspect of our lives. It is more than just a book — it is the foundation of our faith, teaching us how to walk with Christ daily, make wise decisions, and grow in spiritual maturity. Through Scripture, we discover God’s promises, His love, and His plan for humanity.
